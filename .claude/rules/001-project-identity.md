# Dualyss Project Identity Rules

## Brand Identity

**Company**: Dualyss (AIE - Agrupació d'Interès Econòmic)
**Origin**: Catalonia, Europe
**Slogan**: "Protecting democracy with dual deterrence technologies"

## Core Positioning

### Social Message
- **Key concept**: Deterrence (dissuasion)
- Defense of democratic and European values through "preparation"
- Technology as protection mechanism, not aggression

### Industrial Message
- **Key concept**: Enabling capabilities
- European strategic autonomy
- Reindustrialization lever

## Domains of Expertise

1. **Defense** - Unmanned systems, autonomous platforms
2. **Cybersecurity** - Digital infrastructure protection
3. **Biosecurity** - Bio-sanitary response capabilities
4. **Dual Technologies** - Civil + defense applications

## Competitive Advantage

- Deep ecosystem relationships
- Knowledge visualization of the strategic environment
- Integration of SMEs, technology partners, research centers
- Full lifecycle capabilities for critical solutions

## Target Audiences

1. **Institutional** - European programs, government agencies
2. **Industrial** - Defense contractors, tech partners
3. **Academic** - Research centers, universities
4. **Public** - Catalan/European citizens (social narrative)

## Tone of Voice

- **Professional** and **authoritative**
- **Strategic** not militaristic
- **European** perspective
- **Innovation-focused**
- Emphasize **deterrence** over **combat**
- Highlight **protection** and **preparation**
