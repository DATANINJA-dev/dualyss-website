# McKinsey-Style Slide Templates

Ready-to-use patterns for common consulting slides. **Use these as inspiration, not rigid requirements** - adapt the structure, content, and visuals to fit your specific message and audience.

**When to use these templates:**
- You want a proven structure for a common slide type
- You're looking for inspiration on how to present data
- You need to match consulting-style formatting expectations

**When to adapt or skip:**
- Your content doesn't fit neatly into a template
- A simpler approach would be clearer
- Your audience prefers a different style

## Tips for Each Template

1. **Always customize** - Templates are starting points
2. **Action titles first** - Write the message before the content
3. **Data > assertions** - Numbers make arguments
4. **Visual hierarchy** - Guide the eye to key info
5. **Less is more** - Cut until you can't cut anymore
